Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about New Zealand travel

The best time to visit New Zealand is during spring (September-November) and autumn (March-May) when the weather is mild and crowds are smaller. Summer (December-February) is peak season with warmer weather but more tourists.

Chinese citizens need a visa to enter New Zealand. You can apply for a visitor visa online through the New Zealand Immigration website. Processing times vary, so apply well in advance.

New Zealand uses the New Zealand Dollar (NZD). Credit cards are widely accepted, and ATMs are readily available throughout the country.

Yes, English is the primary language spoken in New Zealand. Maori is also an official language but is less commonly used in everyday conversation.

Self-driving is popular in New Zealand with well-maintained roads. There is also an extensive bus network and domestic flights connecting major cities. Trains offer scenic journeys but are less frequent.
